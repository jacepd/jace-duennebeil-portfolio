About Me

Hello! I’m Jace Duennebeil, a dedicated and driven computer science graduate from the University of Utah with a passion for problem-solving, software development, and continuous self-improvement. My journey is defined by a relentless work ethic, a love for learning, and a balanced, healthy lifestyle.

With a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, I have built a strong technical foundation in key areas like algorithms, data structures, mobile app development, and database management. My technical toolkit includes proficiency in C#, C++, Java, Python, Kotlin, SQL, R, and web development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I’m also adept at using development tools like Git, Visual Studio, IntelliJ, Eclipse, and Linux.

Beyond coding, I’m a natural leader and team player. As captain of the University of Utah’s Ultimate Frisbee Club Team, I led strategy development, organized events, and fostered a culture of teamwork. I’m also a professional athlete with the Salt Lake Shred, where I’ve honed my adaptability, discipline, and perseverance on and off the field.

Professionally, I’ve gained valuable experience in customer support and project management. At Microsoft, I served as a Customer Support Specialist, where I enhanced operational efficiency by developing PowerShell scripts and helped IT leaders implement company-wide Microsoft applications. My role as a Service Assistant at Fleming’s Prime Steakhouse sharpened my communication, training, and leadership skills in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment. Additionally, managing over countless successful lawncare projects with Riverview Lawn Care taught me how to independently balance customer satisfaction, scheduling, and quality control.

My personal life reflects the same discipline and commitment. I prioritize health and wellness by maintaining a daily routine of gym workouts, physical activities, and balanced nutrition. This lifestyle fuels my mental clarity and energy, allowing me to excel in both my personal and professional endeavors.

Whether it's developing user-centric software, leading a team to victory, or achieving personal health goals, I approach every challenge with determination and a growth mindset. I’m constantly seeking opportunities to apply my skills, learn new technologies, and make a positive impact. Let's connect and build something amazing together!
